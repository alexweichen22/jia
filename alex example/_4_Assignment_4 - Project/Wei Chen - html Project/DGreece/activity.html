There's Something For Everyone In Greece!

No matter what tickles your fancy, you'll find something to love about this place.

The Nightlife

Part animals unite for a legendary nightlife experience!
Celebrities and locals flock to famous bars and clubs around Greece each evening.
The energy and atmosphere is unmatched and will keep you busy from dusk till dawn!

Party Cities:
  • Athens
  • Mykonos
  • Thessaloniki

Gatronomy

Are you a major foodie? If so, Greece is the place for you!
Have a tradition breakfast in Crete or Epirus.
Sample world famous Mediterranean food, in the local shops or in 5-star restaurants.
Eat freshly caught fish in a tavern or share a meal and homemade wine, Ouzo or Tsipouro with a neighbour.
Whatever culinary experience you're looking for, you'll be sure to find it!

Sun And Sand

Greece is famous for it's many beautful white sand beaches, over 400 of them! From May to October you can expect fun in the sun.
Lounge in a canopy bed or get active with beach volleyball, kayaking, windsurfing and much more!

Some of the best beaches include:
  • Super Paradise Beach, Mykonos
  • Lalaria Beach, Skiathos
  • Sarakiniko Beach, Milos

Adventure

If physical activity is your goal, Greece has got you covered.

Adrenaline junkies can take a motorcycle, atv or car on one of the many off-road dirt routes. Including:
  • The Agrafa mountains
  • Verdant Parnon
  • Evrytania from above: Sela - Fithakia - Ag Vlaherna
  • Asterousia, Crete
Sea and mountain activities may be more to your liking if you preferred a relaxed speed.
  • Sailing for fun and competitively is available widely. Deep sea diving, wind and kite surfing are also popular.
  • Nature lover? The beauty of Greece will astound you peaks and valleys, rivers, lakes, nature trails and forests
